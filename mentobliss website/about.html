Objective

MentoBliss is a stress assessment app that quickly evaluates users' stress levels through a user-friendly questionnaire and advanced sentiment analysis. It offers instant, personalized feedback and recommendations for stress management. With a focus on privacy and community support, MentoBliss aims to empower users to navigate and alleviate stress effectively. The development roadmap includes meticulous planning, rigorous testing, and continuous improvement, ensuring a streamlined and impactful user experience for enhancing mental well-being.

Approach

MentoBliss, an Android app, employs sophisticated sentiment analysis on user responses to gauge stress levels. Through a carefully crafted questionnaire, users provide insights, enabling precise assessment. The app further integrates virtual meetings with psychiatrists, enhancing accessibility to professional support, all seamlessly contained within the Android ecosystem. This ensures a user-friendly and technologically advanced approach to stress management.
